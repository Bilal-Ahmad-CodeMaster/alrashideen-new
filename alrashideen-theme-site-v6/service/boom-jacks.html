Service Overview

Practical Engineering Writing Area

We handle boom jack and lift cylinder work for cranes requiring leakage repair, seal replacement, rod correction, tube inspection or full overhaul. The service focuses on dependable hydraulic performance and smoother boom operation after repair.

Step 01

Opening & Inspection

Cylinder components are opened and checked for scoring, wear, seal damage and straightness issues.

Step 02

Repair & Replacement

Seals, wear parts and damaged internal components are replaced or repaired as required.

Step 03

Assembly & Pressure Test

After assembly, the unit is tested to confirm sealing performance and proper operation.
